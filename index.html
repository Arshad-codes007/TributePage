A short overview of Stan Lee's life

  • Stan Lee, full name 'Stanley Martin Lieber' was born on December 28, 1922 in New York.
  • He lead the expansion of a small publishing house to a multimedia corporation - Marvel Comics that dominated the comics and film industries.
  • He co-created characters including superheroes like Spider-Man, the X-Men, Iron Man, Thor, the Hulk, Ant-Man, the Wasp, the Fantastic Four, Black Panther, Doctor Strange, Scarlet Witch, and Black Widow.
  • He was the very reason why we enjoyed our childhood thinking we all can be superheroes no matter our caste, color and creed.
  • He was very creative and imaginative.
  • He also appeared as a special cameo in many Marvel movies.
  • Stan lee received many awards in his lifetime including 'The Will Eisner Award Hall of Fame', 'Jack Kirby Hall of Fame', 'National Medal of Art' and 'Disney Legends'.
  • This great legend passed away on November 12, 2018 at the age of 95.
